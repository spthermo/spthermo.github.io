Spyridon (Spiros) Thermos

•

I am a computer scientist on a startup quest as a co-founder of Moverse.

•

Previously I was postdoctoral researcher at the University of Edinburgh and adjunct professor (for a year) at the University of Thessaly (UTH). I received my Diploma in computer engineering (2013), my M.Sc. cum laude in computer science (2015), and my Ph.D. from UTH (2020).

•

My research interests lie in the intersection of representation learning and computer vision. Now I'm focusing on generative AI for human motion.
