Spyridon Thermos
sthermos "at" ed.ac.uk

I am a postdoctoral researcher at IDCOM @ The University of Edinburgh working with Sotirios Tsaftaris. I received my PhD from ECE @ University of Thessaly, where I was advised by Gerasimos Potamianos.

In the past, I was a research assistant at VCL, where I worked with Petros Daras who co-advised my PhD.
